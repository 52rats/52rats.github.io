About Us

Burger Boutique is a premier spot to enjoy tastefully crafted artisanal treats. The atmosphere is lively and refined, a combination of modern-industrial architecture and chic décor. We use only the freshest local ingredients for all of our culinary creations. When you dine at Burger Boutique, you can be sure that you're eating healthfully and responsibly. We love our planet and we have rigorous protocols in place to ensure that we recycle as much as we can.
