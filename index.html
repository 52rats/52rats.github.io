Play-Doh

Fun to play with, not to eat

Some cool facts about Play-Doh:

  • More than 3 Billion cans have been sold!
  • Play-Doh originally came in one color only: grey
  • Play-Doh contains wheat, but please don't eat it!
  • You can revive dried-up Play-Doh by adding some water!
  • September 16th is National Play-Doh day
